KYPT

I participated in the Korea Young Physicists’ Tournament, which is a nationwide physics competition held in South Korea. In the competition, I reached Semi-Finalist level in the KYPT along with four other students. The objective of the tournament was to solve numerous physics questions, with an emphasis on experimenting to arrive at solutions. One example of a given question is “The oscillations of a Newton's cradle will gradually decay until the spheres come to rest. Investigate how the rate of decay of a Newton's cradle depends on relevant parameters such as the number, material, and alignment of the spheres.”
