Volatile Organic Compounds (VOC) and Particulate Matter (PM) Adsorption of Alginate Gel Research

Starting from June of 2018, I conducted a research on Particulate Matter (PM) Adsorption of Alginate Gel with one other student. This research was a response to the recent rise in the atmosphere PM concentration in South Korea. Through the research, I investigated for alginate gelâ€™s efficiency in adsorbing Particulate Matter and possible applications of alginate gel as an air purifier in a real life environment. With this research, I earned a silver medal in Chemical Frontier Festival (ChemFF) and a bronze medal in Korea Science Engineering Festival (KSEF). Later, the research was expanded to an investigation for whether alginate gel also adsorbs VOCs. With this expanded research, I earned a gold medal in Genius Olympiad.
