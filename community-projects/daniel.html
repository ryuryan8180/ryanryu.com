Volunteer at Daniel’s School for Disabled

Since July of 2017, I started attending a monthly volunteer program at Daniel’s School for the Disabled, in Seoul, South Korea. Activities we did with the mentally disabled students at the school ranged from teaching the students how to draw to playing outdoor activities. During the year 2018, members in our service program planned to hold an art exhibition made entirely out of artwork created by the disabled students. Our purpose was to promote that mentally disabled students could express their creative minds like everyone else could. To organize this event, we had to reserve a venue, and prepare all the artworks for the exhibition. My role was to teach the students to draw different drawings, like painting with brushes, coloring with crayons, and so on. With the venue and the drawings prepared, we held a successful art exhibition.
